Welcome

We are a Mobile Bartending Service, at Bayside we believe every party is unique and special, we spend the time developing a customized drink menu and make sure our bar decor matches you venue decorations at no extra cost.


Our bartenders have years of experience and are dedicated to crafting quality drinks. We promise to provide freshly made organic Juice and craft syrups for your beverages.

Bayside Summer Empress

This Elegant and refreshing is made with 1908 Empress Gin and is beyond delicious.


Receipe:

  • 2 Oz 1908 Empress Gin (Gin of Preference)
  • 1 1/2 Oz Elderflower Syrup (St. Germain of Preference )
  • 1 Oz Grapefruit (Freshly Squeezed)
  • Garnish with Pansy flower.

    • Enjoy!

    Blacktart Blackberry Martini

    This martini is one of my favorites. It's Sweet and tart, packed with lots of antioxidants.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Vodka St. George (Vodka of Preference)
  • 1 Oz Simple
  • 3-4 Blackberries (Muddled)
  • 1 Oz Lemon Juice ( Freshly Squeezed)
  • Garnish with blackberry and Lemon twist.


    • Enjoy!
