Spring Tea

Spring is here! We crafted a delicious spring inspired drink made with Julie's 'Flower Power Tea'.


Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Grey Goose (Vodka of Preference)
  • 1 .5 Oz Elderflower Syrup (St. Germain of Preference )
  • 1.5 Oz Meyer Lemon Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 2-4 Oz Juie's 'Flower Power Tea' Julies Tea
  • Garnish: Dry Flower Medley.
