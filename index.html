Sweetini Grapefruit Martini

A twist to a martini, this sweetini is packed with a refeshing sweetness.


Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Vodka St. George (Vodka of Preference)
  • 1 1/2 Oz Simple
  • 1 Oz Grapefruit (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Grenadine
  • Garnish with grapefruit wedge.


    • Enjoy!

    Blacktart Blackberry Martini

    This martini is one of my favorites. It's Sweet and tart, packed with lots of antioxidants.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Vodka St. George (Vodka of Preference)
  • 1 Oz Simple
  • 3-4 Blackberries (Muddled)
  • 1 Oz Lemon Juice ( Freshly Squeezed)
  • Garnish with blackberry and Lemon twist.


    • Enjoy!

    The Brazil Caipirinha Cocktail

    This classic Caipirinha is not so ordinary. It is made with the best Cachaça.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Avua Prata Cachaça (or Preference Cachaça)
  • 1 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Dark Sugar
  • 2-3 Lime halves (Muddled)
  • Garnish with lime wheel.


    • Enjoy!

    The Refresher Mojito

    This amazing mojito on the rocks will bring cuba to your lounge.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Light Rum Bacardi (or Preference Light Rum)
  • 1 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Simple
  • 3-4 Limes (Muddled)
  • 2-3 Big Mint Leaves (Muddled)
  • Garnish with Lime Wheel and Mint Sprig.


    • Enjoy!

    "Not So Classic" Margarita On the rocks margarita

    Did you say Margarita? This margarita combines the sweetness of agave and tartness of limes.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Lunazul (or Preference Tequila)
  • 1 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Light Agave Nectar (Mohadva Light)
  • Garnish with Lime Wheel.
  • Rim with Tajin and Agave Nectar


    • Enjoy!

    The Swirl Strawberry Mango Margarita

    Did you say Strawberry Mango? This blended margarita is by far one of the greatest combos I could had ever thought of.


    Receipe:

  • 2 Oz Tequila Espolon Reposado (or Preference Tequila)
  • 1 Oz Light Agave Nectar (Mohadva Light)
  • 4 Strawberries (Blend)
  • 2-3 Mango Slices (Blend)
  • Garnish with Strawberry and Mango Slice.
  • Rim with Tajin


    • Enjoy!

    Herbal Revival A Mocktail

    This Mocktail is the best Herbal drink that won't taste like medicine. Carefully crafte to bring the garden to your cocktail.


    Receipe:

  • 1 1/2 Seedlip Herbal 108
  • Dash of Angostura Bitters (Muddle)
  • 1/2 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1/2 Oz Simple
  • 1 Brown sugar cube (Muddle)
  • 2 Big mint leaves (Muddle)
  • 2-3 Small Lime Wedges in cup
  • Garnish with mint sprig.


    • Enjoy!
