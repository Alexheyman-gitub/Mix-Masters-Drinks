Herbal Revival A Mocktail

This Mocktail is the best Herbal drink that won't taste like medicine. Carefully crafte to bring the garden to your cocktail.


Receipe:

  • 1 1/2 Seedlip Herbal 108
  • Dash of Angostura Bitters (Muddle)
  • 1/2 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1/2 Oz Simple
  • 1 Brown sugar cube (Muddle)
  • 2 Big mint leaves (Muddle)
  • 2-3 Small Lime Wedges in cup
  • Garnish with mint sprig.


    • Enjoy!

    Angustifolia Martini
    A sweet & Tart delight

    This martini is one of my favorites, it combines the sweet aroma of lavender and tart flavors of lemon, so refreshing.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Oz Vodka (St George Vodka)
  • 1 Oz Lemon Juice (freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 1/2 Oz Laveder Simple
  • Dash of Lavender Bitters (Scrappy's)

    Sweet Luna Margarita
    Sweet and Salty

    This mouth watering margarita is a cinco de mayo everyday! Cant't go wrong with a classic margarita, but at Bayside Bartender we have crafted the perfect sweet and salty combo.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Oz Tequila Espolon Reposado (or Preference Tequila)
  • 1 Oz Lime Juice (Freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Light Agave Nectar (Mohadva Light)
  • Garnish with Lime Wheel.
  • Rim with Tajin and Agave Nectar


    • ~Enjoy~


    5 Cent lemonade
    Lavender Lemonade

    Summer time lemonades all around. I call this drink The 5 cent lemonade because it reminds me of the ingredients I already had at home, a beautiful lemon tree and lavender in my garden.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Oz Vodka (St George Vodka)
  • 1 Oz Lemon Juice (freshly Squeezed)
  • 1 Oz Lavender Simple (prepare ahead with dry sprigs)
  • Dash of Lavender Bitters (Scrappy's)
  • Add a splash of Sparkling Mineral water (do not shake in mixer)
  • Garnish with lemon wheel & Lavender Sprig

    • ~Enjoy~
