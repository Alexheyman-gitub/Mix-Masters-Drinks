A Bayside Bartender story

I wanted to write this short passage on how I started my company Bayside Bartender. It was early 2019 I had been struggling to find work as Web developer, so I became a ride share driver on the side, when one mid day a passanger jumped in. I realized she was dressed kind of awesone and she had this amazing personality. I asked her "Going to work"? she replied "Yes I am a bartender". I said "That's awesome, I wanted to learn to become a bartender, but the school I wanted to train at costs too much. she then replied "OMG really, that's ridiculous, you don't need to go to school, you just have to teach yourself. so then I said " so...I can just teach my self? I mean I wouldn't even know where to start? Is the licsence expensive? She then sais "Alex the test is super easy and the cost are like $15.00 and all you need to do is buy bartending tools". I honestly had no idea what she was talking about but I was interested tho. She then saids "Alex this is a Jigger and you can measure liquor with this, Good Luck"! I shouted Thanks!! as she rushed into her bar. And after much trial and error, I worked at gigs being a bartender for a full year and after much doubt in 2021 I fully launched Bayside Bartender.

At Bayside we believe every event is special we take our time to craft special bar menus and make sure our decor is on point. We maintain professionalism and friendly service, we are diverse and inclusiven and treat everyone with respect. we believe in providing long lasting memories, we are dedicated to crafting delicious drinks with high quality ingredients. We are Bayside Bartender.

We offer free bartending classes & ABC Licsence Certification. If you or you know someone who might be considering becoming a bartender please share our website, we are currently working on our blog and will offer tips from experienced bartenders.

Cheers!
